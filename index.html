About

Passionate about the concept of innovation and creating a future for people where their quality of life enhanced through this idea of perpetual advancement, I've found that the world of tech is at the forefront of it all. With this sentiment, I've become a software engineer in hopes to make products for people everywhere that directly impact their daily lives and radically change how they live their lives.

Through my journey of becoming a software engineer, I've built up a skillset in Python, Ruby on Rails, and JavaScript. I've also built up experience with React/Redux and the MERN stack. Echoing my passion of innovation, I'm constantly seeking to learn new things, whether it be a new language, framework, or just ridiculous programming trivia.

On a personal note, this everlasting curiosity lends itself to other facets of my life, leading me down rabbit holes of topics that I can easily lose hours and hours of my day on. Whether it be exploring new foods throughout the city of NYC or reinforcing my skills in a particular foreign language that I'm trying to learn, I am always just looking to learn.

By the way, check out my work here.
